About SmartLib

SmartLib helps organizations transform document collections into searchable, AI-ready knowledge hubs powered by retrieval-augmented generation.

The platform combines Azure OpenAI, Docling, PyMuPDF, and other open-source projects to deliver secure ingestion, vector indexing, and conversational access to enterprise content. SmartLib is distributed under the MIT License and publishes its source repository to satisfy the obligations of all bundled open-source software.
