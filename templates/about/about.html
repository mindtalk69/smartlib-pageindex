SmartLib helps organizations transform document collections into searchable, AI-ready knowledge hubs powered by retrieval-augmented generation.

The platform combines private source and open-source projects to deliver secure ingestion, vector indexing, and conversational access from small to enterprise content.

Key Features
Easy Ingestion

Seamless document upload and processing pipeline.

Secure & Compliant

Robust data privacy controls and licensing tracking.

AI Chat

Intuitive chat interface for knowledge discovery.

Multi-LLM Support

Flexible integration with various LLM providers.
