About SmartLib

SmartLib helps organizations transform document collections into searchable, AI-ready knowledge hubs powered by retrieval-augmented generation.

The platform combines private source and open-source projects to deliver secure ingestion, vector indexing, and conversational access from small to enterprise content. Key features include: - Easier document upload and processing, - Robust data privacy and compliance controls, - AI-powered chat interface for knowledge discovery, - support for multiple LLM providers, - Scalable vector database management,
